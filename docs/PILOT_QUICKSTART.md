# PitchSide AI - Pilot Quick Start Guide

Welcome to PitchSide AI! This guide will help you get started with your club's AI-powered command center.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. First Login
1. Visit the app URL provided in your welcome email
2. Click **Sign Up** and enter your email and password
3. Check your email for a verification link
4. After verification, sign in to access your dashboard

### 2. Welcome Tour
- On first login, you'll see a **Welcome Modal**
- Click **Start Tour** for a guided walkthrough of key features
- Or click **Skip** to explore on your own

---

## Core Features

### Squad Management
**Location:** Squad tab (Players icon)

- **Add Players:** Click "+ Add Player" to create player profiles
- **Player Stats:** Each player has a radar chart showing key attributes
- **AI Analysis:** View AI-generated performance insights for each player

### Fixture Management
**Location:** Match tab (Calendar icon)

- **Schedule Fixtures:** Click "+ Schedule Fixture" to add upcoming matches
- **Match Prep:** Click "Hype Pack" to generate pre-match content
- **Post-Match:** Generate match reports after completed games

### AI Content Generation
**Location:** Content tab (FileText icon)

- **Generate Content:** Click "AI Generate" to create match previews, reports, and social posts
- **Workflow:** Content moves through Draft → Approved → Published stages
- **AI Graphics:** Generate matchday images with various styles (Hype, Minimal, Retro, Neon)

### Sponsor Management
**Location:** Settings → Sponsors

- **Add Sponsors:** Track sponsor partnerships with tier levels (Platinum/Gold/Silver)
- **ROI Tracking:** Monitor exposure value and engagement metrics
- **PDF Reports:** Generate professional sponsor value reports

### Availability Tracking
**Location:** Availability tab (UserCheck icon)

- **Track Squad Availability:** Mark players as Available/Unavailable/Maybe/Injured
- **Fixture-based:** Availability is tracked per upcoming fixture
- **Quick Overview:** See summary counts at a glance

## Quick Start Checklist

Your dashboard shows a **Quick Start Checklist** to guide setup:

1. **Add your first player** - Build your squad roster
2. **Schedule a fixture** - Add an upcoming match
3. **Generate content** - Create your first AI content
4. **Explore admin settings** - Configure platform preferences

The checklist disappears once all items are complete!

---

## Tips for Success

### Content Workflow
1. Schedule fixture → 2. Generate "Hype Pack" → 3. Review in Content tab → 4. Approve & Publish

### Best Practices
- Add complete player profiles for better AI analysis
- Schedule fixtures at least a week ahead for optimal content planning
- Review AI-generated content before publishing
- Keep sponsor data updated for accurate ROI tracking
